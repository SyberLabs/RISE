## PURPOSE

This document catalogs **open-access, public domain, and Creative Commons** visual resources suitable for RISE Chamber sessions.

All sources are:

- **Legally reproducible** (public domain, CC0, CC-BY, or CC-BY-SA)
- **Visually resonant** with Neohumanist aesthetics
- **Diagrammatically rich** (not merely decorative)
- **Available digitally** for processing and display
