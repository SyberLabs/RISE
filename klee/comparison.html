🎨 Aesthetic Analysis

The Problem (LEFT):

  • Blocky forms "sleeve" organic lines - creates visual tension
  • Grid-aligned rectangles feel mechanical, not natural
  • 1D noise creates predictable "wiggly" patterns
  • Forms don't follow the natural flow of lines

The Solution (RIGHT):

  • Smooth contours complement organic lines - visual harmony
  • Marching squares extracts natural boundaries from density field
  • 2D multi-octave Simplex noise creates rich, unpredictable textures
  • Bezier curve smoothing produces Klee-like elegant forms
  • Forms emerge naturally from line intersections, not grid artifacts

Technical Improvements:

  • 2D Simplex Noise: Uses actual (x,y) position, not just step counter - creates spatial coherence
  • Multi-octave: Combines 3 frequencies for richer detail (like music harmonics)
  • Marching Squares: Computer graphics algorithm for extracting iso-contours
  • Contour Smoothing: Quadratic Bezier curves for elegant boundaries
  • Higher grid resolution: 100×100 vs 50×50 for finer detail
