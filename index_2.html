Visual Interlocution generates brief, subliminal flashes of light and color patterns during your session. These interrupts are designed to enhance the consciousness experience but may not be suitable for everyone.

This feature may cause issues for individuals with:

  • Photosensitive epilepsy
  • Seizure disorders

  • Configurable duration (16-200ms)
  • Can be disabled at any time
  • Respects system photosensitivity preferences
