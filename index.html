Photosensitivity Warning

Rhythmic Visuals introduces brief flashes and visual exposures between moments of reading. Presence can range from 150 milliseconds to 2 seconds. The system limits rapid recurrence, but high-contrast visual changes may still affect photosensitive individuals.

This feature may cause issues for individuals with:

  • Photosensitive epilepsy or seizure disorders
  • Migraine sensitivity to light
  • Visual processing sensitivities
  • Motion sickness susceptibility

Engineering limits and controls:

  • Rapid recurrence remains constrained even when Frequency is 100%
  • Presence is configurable from 150 milliseconds to 2 seconds
  • Rhythmic visuals can be disabled at any time during the session
  • The global Photosensitivity setting completely disables these visuals

These engineering measures are designed to reduce risk; they are not a medical safeguard or guarantee.

By enabling this feature, you acknowledge that you have read this warning and accept responsibility for any effects. If you experience discomfort, dizziness, or visual disturbances, disable this feature immediately.
