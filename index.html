Photosensitivity Warning

Visual Interlocution generates brief, subliminal flashes of light and color patterns during your session. These interrupts are designed to enhance the consciousness experience but may not be suitable for everyone.

This feature may cause issues for individuals with:

  • Photosensitive epilepsy or seizure disorders
  • Migraine sensitivity to light
  • Visual processing sensitivities
  • Motion sickness susceptibility

Safety protections in place:

  • Maximum frequency limited to prevent rapid flashing
  • Configurable duration (16-200ms per flash)
  • Can be disabled at any time during session
  • Respects system accessibility preferences

By enabling this feature, you acknowledge that you have read this warning and accept responsibility for any effects. If you experience discomfort, dizziness, or visual disturbances, disable this feature immediately.
