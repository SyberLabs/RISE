About This Engine

The Abstract Figure Engine generates minimalist abstract figures inspired by Japanese Zen rock gardens (karesansui). Each composition is procedurally created from overlapping rock-like shapes that coalesce into a singular sculptural form, embodying principles of wabi-sabi (imperfect beauty), asymmetry, and contemplative negative space.

Aesthetic Principles

Wabi-Sabi: Embracing imperfection through random perturbations and organic irregularities.

Ma (Negative Space): Sparse composition emphasizing emptiness as part of the design.

Asymmetry: Dynamic balance through non-uniform placement and biased positioning.

Simplicity: Monochromatic palette and minimal elements creating contemplative forms.
