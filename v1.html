🎯 Getting Started

Three ways to begin:

⚡ Temporal Controls

Pacing (WPM) — Words per minute. Start at 220, increase as you adapt. Range: 100-800.

Chunking Modes:

  • Word — Single word display (recommended for beginners)
  • Phrase — 2-4 words grouped by natural phrases
  • Sentence — Full sentences (advanced, slower pace)

Pacing Curves:

  • Flat — Constant speed throughout
  • Exponential — Gradual acceleration
  • Induction — Slow start, rapid middle, slow end (Gateway-inspired)
  • Sine — Rhythmic wave pattern

Focus — Light binaural beats (12 Hz Alpha waves) for concentration

Deep — Theta waves (6 Hz) with harmonics for deeper states

Gateway — Full entrainment with layered frequencies (inspired by Monroe Institute)

⚠️ Binaural beats require headphones to be effective.

🔐 Privacy & Safety

Privacy: All processing happens in your browser. No data is sent to servers.

Safety:

  • Start at lower speeds (150-220 WPM) and increase gradually
  • Take breaks every 15-20 minutes
  • If you experience discomfort, reduce speed or disable visuals
  • Binaural beats are not suitable for everyone (epilepsy, heart conditions)
💡 Tips for Best Results
  1. Environment: Dim lighting, quiet space, comfortable posture
  2. Headphones: Required for binaural audio to work
  3. Focus Point: Keep eyes on center, let peripheral vision catch words
  4. Don't Subvocalize: Trust your visual cortex, resist urge to "sound out" words
  5. Progressive Adaptation: Speed increases feel uncomfortable at first, then natural
  6. Intention: Set a clear purpose before entering (absorption, meditation, exploration)
