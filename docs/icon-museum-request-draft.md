# Draft — permission request to the Icon Museum and Study Center

To: Registrar, The Icon Museum and Study Center (via the contact route
on iconmuseum.org — the Photo Policy page directs image licensing
questions to the Registrar)

Subject: Permission request — displaying collection images in a
non-commercial contemplative reading application

---

Dear Registrar,

I am the developer of R.I.S.E., a free, non-commercial reading
environment for slow, contemplative reading. One of its rooms is
devoted to Scripture, where a small set of sacred images — each a
specific museum work, displayed with full attribution — accompanies
the reading as a still, centered focal image.

I am writing to ask permission to display images of works from your
collection in this setting, beginning with:

- **Christ Pantocrator**, Russia, late 19th century, oil on wood
  (accession R2017.4, gift of Dmitry Feld)

Each image would be shown with complete attribution (title, origin,
date, accession number, and "The Icon Museum and Study Center,
Clinton, Massachusetts"), linked to the object's page on your
website. The application is free to use, carries no advertising, and
sells nothing; images are displayed for contemplative reading only
and are not redistributed, edited, or offered for download.

If you are able to grant this, a short written confirmation — even a
reply to this email stating the permission and any conditions — is
all we need; we record the stated basis alongside every image we
display, and we would be glad to honor any attribution wording or
usage conditions you specify. If there are other works from the
collection you would be open to including on the same terms, we would
welcome that gladly — your collection is exactly the tradition this
room hopes to honor.

Thank you for your stewardship of these works and for considering
this request.

With appreciation,

[NAME]
[contact email]
R.I.S.E. — https://rise-v2-symbolic-experience.netlify.app

---
